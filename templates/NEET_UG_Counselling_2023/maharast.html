Maharashtra MBBS Counselling 2023: Counselling Process


Maharashtra MBBS Counselling 2023 is a state-centralised online counselling process. The sole authority to conduct online NEET UG Counselling 2023 is Maharashtra CET i.e. MH CET, Mumbai.


Candidates need to visit the official website at https://cetcell.mahacet.org for registration based on NEET UG 2023 Score. Candidates can apply to MBBS/BDS Courses in various Government/Private medical colleges in the state of Maharashtra.


In order to secure an MBBS seat in deemed medical college, Candidates will have to register at https://mcc.nic.in. There are four rounds of counselling that take place followed by a stray vacancy round of NEET Counselling 2023 is subject to the availability of MBBS/BDS Seats after the Mop-up round. 


Admissions to the Medical/Dental/Ayush colleges of Maharashtra are solely based on the NEET 2023 score. Candidates can participate in the Maharashtra NEET Counselling 2023 to get admission to MBBS/BDS/BAMS/BHMS/BUMS/BSMS/Paramedical programs. Details are shared in the article below.

Registration:


  • Candidates have to register on the website i.e. https://cetcell.mahacet.org and submit the counselling fee which is Rs. 1000/5000/6000 through Internet banking or any online mode.


  • MHCET Mumbai will publish the Maharashtra NEET 2023 merit list for the qualified candidate as per the secured marks and required percentile. Selected candidates will be allowed to participate in further events of Counselling.

Choice Filling:


  • Candidates need to fill in Online choices at https://cetcell.mahacet.org  by using their registration number and password generated at the time of registration.


  • Candidates have to fill in their choices of colleges as per their priority. They can fill choices as many as they wish.

Allotment Result:


  • Candidates can check the seat allotment status from the CET Mumbai at https://cetcell.mahacet.org.  


  • Candidates are advised to download their allotment letter, which must be produced when reporting to the allotted college.


  • Seats are allotted to the candidates on the basis of rank, scored marks, and the choices of their preferences.

Reporting to Allotted College:


  • This is the final event when the candidates need to report to the allotted college with an allotment letter before the last date of admission.


  • Seats will be considered as confirmed, once the fee payment and verification of documents will take place.

Maharashtra MBBS Counselling 2023: Eligibility Criteria


  • Candidates must have qualified for NEET UG 2023.

  • Candidates must have the domicile of Maharashtra (Differ for NRI/Other Quota)

  • Candidates must have passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and English as Core subjects with a minimum of 50% marks, 40% marks are required for SC/ST Category candidates.

  • The candidates should have completed 17 years of age as of Dec 31st, 2023. Candidates born on or after Jan 2nd, 2006, will not be entertained.

Maharashtra MBBS Counselling 2023: Allotment Procedure


As per the merit list prepared by MHCET Mumbai, shortlisted candidates will be called for Maharashtra MBBS Counselling 2023. The following sequence of events has to be followed in the counselling process to secure an MBBS/BDS seat in the medical institutes of Maharashtra. 


  • Participating candidates need to register themselves on the Online Portal of Maharashtra MBBS Medical Admission 2023 at https://cetcell.mahacet.org.


  • Post-registration on the counselling portal of Maharashtra, candidates will be asked to fill in their preferences of colleges.


  • Based on the choices filled and seat matrix MHCET Mumbai releases the provisional merit list followed by the final Maharashtra UG medical seat allotment list.


  • Further candidates will have to submit the part academic fee to confirm their seats.


  • Candidates, whose names are included in the seat allotment of Maharashtra NEET UG 2023 Medical/Dental will be required to download their allotment letter from the MHCET Portal at https://cetcell.mahacet.org.   


  • Shortlisted candidates are then required to report to the allotted college for payment of the balance admission fee, document verification, and reserving the allotted medical/dental admission seat.
