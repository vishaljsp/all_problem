Madhya Pradesh MBBS Counselling 2023: Counselling Process


Madhya Pradesh MBBS Counselling 2023 is a state-centralised online counselling process. The sole authority to conduct online MP MBBS Counselling 2023 is the Department of Medical Education, Govt. of Madhya Pradesh Bhopal.


Candidates need to visit the official website at https://dme.mponline.gov.in for registration based on NEET UG Score 2023. Candidates can apply to MBBS/BDS Courses in various Government/Private medical colleges in the state of Madhya Pradesh based on NEET UG 2023 score followed by the counselling conducted by DME Bhopal.

Registration:


  • Candidates have to register on the website i.e. https://dme.mponline.gov.in and submit the registration fee which is Rs. 1000 through Internet banking or any online mode.


  • DME Bhopal will publish the Madhya Pradesh MBBS 2023 state merit list for the qualified candidate as per the secured marks and required percentile. Selected candidates will be allowed to participate in further events of Counselling.

Choice Filling:


  • Candidates need to fill in Online choices at https://dme.mponline.gov.in by using their registration number and password generated at the time of registration.


  • Candidates have to fill in their choices of colleges as per their priority. They can fill choices as many as they wish.

Allotment Result:


  • Candidates can check the seat allotment status from the Department of Medical Education, Bhopal at https://dme.mponline.gov.in.  


  • Candidates must download their allotment letter to produce at the time of reporting to the allotted college.


  • Seats are allotted to the candidates based on rank, scored marks, and the choices of their preferences.

Reporting to Allotted College:


  • This is the final event when the candidates need to report to the allotted college with an allotment letter before the last date of admission.


  • Seats will be considered as confirmed, once the fee payment and verification of documents will be taken place.

Madhya Pradesh MBBS Counselling 2023: Eligibility Criteria


  • Candidates must have qualified for NEET-UG 2023.

  • Candidates must have a domicile of Madhya Pradesh (Differ for Management/NRI Quota)

  • Candidates must have passed 12th from any recognized board.

  • Candidates must have passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and English as Core subjects with a minimum of 50% marks, 40% marks are required for SC/ST Category candidates.

  • The candidates should have completed 17 years of age as of Dec 31st, 2023. Candidates born on or after Jan 2nd, 2006, will not be entertained.
