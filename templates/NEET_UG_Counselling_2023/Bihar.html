Bihar MBBS Counselling 2023: Counselling Process


Bihar MBBS Counselling 2023 is a state centralised online counselling process. The sole authority to conduct online NEET UG Counselling 2023 is Bihar Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board i.e. BCECE, Patna.


Candidates need to visit the official website at https://bceceboard.bihar.gov.in for registration based on NEET UG Score 2023. Candidates can apply to MBBS/BDS Courses in various Government/Private medical colleges in the state of Bihar.


For All India Quota seats in MBBS/BDS in medical colleges, Candidates will have to register at https://mcc.nic.in. There are four rounds of counselling that will take place followed by a stray vacancy round of NEET UG Counselling 2023 is subject to the availability of MBBS/BDS Seats after the mop-up round.


Admissions to the Medical/Dental/Ayush colleges of Bihar are solely based on the NEET UG 2023 score. Detailed information is shared in the article below.


Registration: 


By entering NEET UG 2023 Roll no. and Date of Birth followed by a verification link to provide registered mobile no. and e-mail id, candidates can register for Bihar MBBS Counselling 2023 at https://bceceboard.bihar.gov.in. The candidate must keep the User Id and password confidential. The registered e-mail id provided by the candidate will be the User Id for further communication. 

Personal Details:


After successful registration, candidates need to sign in to the account created and fill in the required personal details. Click on the “Save & Continue” button. The details comprised will be the name, parents' name, academic details, address, and other relevant details of the candidate.

Upload Photo & Signature:


Following providing personal details, candidates then need to upload a photo as per the required dimension and a bilingual signature (Hindi & English) after scanning the same. After uploading, click on the “Save & Continue” button to move to the next tab.

Educational Details:


After uploading a signature and photograph, candidates then have to fill in the educational details as per the requirement. Then click on the “Save & Continue” button again.

Preview Application:


This is the last step before paying the counselling fee. Candidates can edit the details they provided and upload them. They need to click on the “Back to Edit” button. Thereafter “Confirm & Submit” button to finally register on the BCECE Portal at https://bceceboard.bihar.gov.in.

Counselling Fee:


To ensure successful registration, candidates need to pay the counselling fee. For that, they must click on the “Proceed to Payment” button. There are multiple options being facilitated by the BCECE Board Patna. Candidates can either deposit Bank Challan offline or can pay Online mode through Credit Card/Debit Card/ Internet Banking.

Bihar MBBS Counselling 2023: Eligibility Criteria


  • Candidates must have qualified for NEET-UG 2023.

  • Candidates must have the domicile of Bihar (Differ for Management/NRI Quota)

  • Candidates must have passed 12th from a recognized board

  • Candidates must have attained 17 years of age on Dec 31st, 2023 (born on or after 31st Dec 2006)

Bihar MBBS Counselling 2023: Required Documents
